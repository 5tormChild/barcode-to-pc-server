If the app doesn't find the computer

Perhaps your firewall and/or antivirus is blocking the communication between the server and the app, try to temporaely disable it or try to add Barcode to PC to the exceptions list.

If you're not sure how to do it take a look here: Turn Windows Firewall on or off
